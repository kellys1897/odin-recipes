Korean Garlic Cream Cheese Bread

Not me simping over bread..

This cream cheese garlic bread became popular in the streets of Korea and went viral online recently this past year. The bread's has everything.. It's crispy, soft, chewy, garlicky, buttery, sweet and savory! Try not to get addicted..

Ingredients

  • Dinner rolls
  • 4 oz of cream cheese(softened)
  • 2 tbsp. sugar
  • 1/4 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp. milk
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp of chopped parsley
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Place rolls on baking sheet lined with parchment paper
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  3. In a bowl, add softened cream cheese and beat with electric mixer until creamy. Add sugar and mix. Finally, add heavy cream and beat until smooth.
  4. Add the sweet cream cheese mixture to a piping bag or a ziploc bag with the corner cut.
  5. Cut the rolls into 6-8 equal wedges like a pie but do not cut all the way through. Put the cream cheese mixture into the rolls through the slices.
  6. In a bowl, add melted butter with the egg and milk. Whisk well until smooth. Add the parsley and minced garlic.
  7. Dip your rolls into the garlic butter mixture to coat the top and let it sit.
  8. Place the rolls on the baking sheet. You can also add parmesan cheese on top to your liking.
  9. Bake at 350 degrees F for 5-10 minutes or until it is golden! Enjoy!
